## Hyperlinks or cross-references
Hyperlinks or cross-references.

### Adding Hyperlinks

Put the text to display in hard brackets [] followed by the url in parentheses ()   
[Display Text](URL)

Fill the details and get the text hyperlinked to open in a new tab.

[Visit website: Correct Link](https://techwriterstribe.com)

Wrong:

[Visit website: Wrong link](www.techwriterstribe.com)
