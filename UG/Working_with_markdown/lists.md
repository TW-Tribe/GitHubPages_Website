## Lists
Using ordered and unordered lists.

*Type of Lists*
- *Unordered:* Use * with space before the list item.   
- *Ordered:* Use number 1 with space before the numbered item. 
- *Nested list:* Press tab for alignment and then use * or number

### Unordered
You can add the following to the document:
* Images
* Videos
* Hyperlinks
* Cross references

### Ordered

A document structure contains the following in TOC:
1. Top content   
    a. Title   
    b. Copyright   
    c. TOC   
    d. Glossary
1. Body Content   
    a. Chapter (Heading 1)   
    b. Sub-sections/sub-chapters (Heading 2)   
    c. Topics (Heading 3)
1. End Content   
    a. Appendix   
    b. Index

### Nested

A document structure contains the following in TOC:
1. Top content   
    a. Title   
    b. Copyright   
    c. TOC   
    d. Glossary
1. Body Content   
    a. Chapter (Heading 1)   
    b. Sub-sections/sub-chapters (Heading 2)   
    c. Topics (Heading 3)
1. End Content   
    - Appendix
    - Index
